KIND Foundation

The KIND Foundation is a 501(e)(3) organisation established by KIND that aims to foster kinder and more empathetic communities. Its mission is
brought to life throgh a variety of programming meant to celebrate and inspire positive action. In 2016, through a programm called KIND People,
The KIND Foundation celebrated seven individuals transforming their communities through kindness with $1.1MM in prizes. Each month, the
Foundation also awards one socially-impactful cause with a $10,000 grant. In 2017, it will help young people to build, kinder more inclusive
